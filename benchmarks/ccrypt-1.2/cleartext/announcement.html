VNC under new ownership!

In January 1999, AT&T acquired the lab founded 12 years earlier as Olivetti Research Laboratory, and more recently known as ORL. 

VNC is therefore now a project of AT&T Laboratories Cambridge, and we look forward to its continued development and the new opportunities that this partnership presents.
